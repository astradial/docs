## What is a user?

A user is someone on your team who needs to use Astradial. Think of it like giving someone a desk and a phone in an office. Once you add them, they get their own login, their own extension number, and they can start making and receiving calls.

## How to add a user

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Users page">
    In the sidebar, go to **Configure** and then click **Users**. This is where you see everyone on your team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the add button">
    Click the **+ Add User** button at the top of the page. A form will open up.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in their details">
    Fill in the following fields:

    - **Username** -- a short name they will use to log in (e.g. `john` or `sarah.m`)
    - **Email** -- their email address
    - **Full Name** -- their real name (e.g. Sarah Miller)
    - **Extension** -- a short number like `1001`, `1002`, or `1003`
    - **Password** -- a password for their account

    <Tip>
      Think of the extension like an internal phone number. When someone inside your organisation wants to call Sarah, they just dial her extension (e.g. 1001) instead of a full phone number.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Create">
    Hit the **Create** button. That is it -- the user is now part of your organisation and can start receiving calls.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The users table

After you add people, they all show up in a table on the Users page. You can see at a glance:

| Column    | What it shows                                      |
| --------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| Extension | Their internal phone number (e.g. 1001)            |
| Name      | Their full name                                    |
| Email     | Their email address                                |
| Role      | What they are allowed to do (Owner, Admin, etc.)   |
| Status    | Whether their account is active or not             |

<Info>
  You can edit or remove a user at any time by clicking on them in the table.
</Info>

## Tips

<Note>
  Each extension number must be unique. You cannot give two people the same extension, just like you cannot give two desks the same phone number.
</Note>

<Warning>
  Only **Owners** and **Admins** can add new users. If you do not see the **+ Add User** button, ask your organisation's admin to add you or upgrade your role.
</Warning>
