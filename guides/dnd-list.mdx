The DND (Do Not Disturb) list prevents specific phone numbers from being contacted by your campaigns and automation flows.

## Viewing the DND list

The table displays all blocked numbers with:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Phone number** | The blocked number |
| **Status** | Active or inactive |
| **Actions** | Delete button to remove the number |

## Adding a number

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Add DND">
    Click the **Add DND** button in the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the phone number">
    Type the phone number to block in the dialog.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Click **Add** to add the number to both your personal and the global DND registry.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Removing a number

Click the trash icon on any row to remove the number from your personal DND list.

<Info>
  Numbers added to the DND list are registered in both a global registry (shared across all users) and your personal list. Removing a number only affects your personal list.
</Info>

<Warning>
  Ensure your DND list is up to date to comply with Do Not Call regulations. The system checks this list before initiating any outbound calls or messages.
</Warning>
