Understanding your daily call capacity helps you plan campaigns effectively and choose the right number of channels for your business needs.

## How it works

When you make outbound calls through Astra Dial, each **channel** represents one simultaneous phone line. The number of calls you can make per day depends on three factors:

1. **Number of channels** — how many simultaneous calls your plan supports
2. **Average call duration** — how long each connected call lasts
3. **Working hours** — the window during which calls are placed

## The math behind it

Each channel goes through a **call cycle** for every attempt:

| Phase | Duration | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Dispatch | ~2s | DND check + call initiation |
| Ringing | ~20s | Phone rings until pickup or timeout |
| Conversation | varies | Actual talk time (if picked up) |

<Note>
  The ring timeout is configured at **20 seconds** by default. If the customer doesn't pick up within this window, the call ends and the channel is freed for the next call.
</Note>

### For calls that are not picked up

```
Cycle time = 2s (dispatch) + 20s (ring timeout) = ~22 seconds
```

### For calls that are picked up

```
Cycle time = 2s (dispatch) + ring time + conversation duration
```

Since not every call is answered, the **effective cycle time** is a weighted average based on your pickup rate.

### Typical scenario

With a **40% pickup rate** and **20 second average talk time**:

- 60% of calls → not picked → 22s per cycle
- 40% of calls → picked → ~30s per cycle (2s dispatch + 8s avg ring + 20s talk)

**Weighted average: ~25 seconds per call**

```
Calls per channel per hour = 3600 / 25 = ~144 calls
```

## Capacity reference table

Based on **10 working hours** (e.g., 9 AM to 7 PM) and a typical call mix:

| Channels | Calls per day | Best for |
|---|---|---|
| 5 | ~7,000 - 8,000 | Small teams, targeted outreach |
| 10 | ~14,000 - 16,000 | Growing teams, regional campaigns |
| 15 | ~22,000 - 24,000 | Mid-size operations |
| 20 | ~30,000 - 33,000 | Large-scale campaigns |
| 30 | ~45,000 - 49,000 | High-volume operations |
| 50 | ~75,000 - 82,000 | Enterprise-grade outreach |

<Info>
  These are conservative estimates. Actual throughput can be **higher** if your DND filter rate is high (DND-filtered contacts are skipped instantly, freeing up the channel in under 1 second) or if your average call duration is shorter.
</Info>

## Factors that increase throughput

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="High DND filter rate" icon="shield">
    Contacts flagged as DND are skipped instantly without placing a call. A 15-20% DND rate means those channels are freed in milliseconds, boosting effective throughput by 15-20%.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Shorter call duration" icon="clock">
    If your average conversation is 10 seconds instead of 20, each channel handles significantly more calls per hour.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Extended working hours" icon="sun">
    Running campaigns for 12 hours instead of 10 gives you a 20% increase in daily capacity.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Clean contact lists" icon="list-check">
    Fewer invalid numbers means less time wasted on failed call attempts.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Quick formula

To estimate your capacity for any configuration:

```
Daily calls = Channels × (3600 / Avg cycle seconds) × Working hours
```

**Example**: 20 channels, 25s avg cycle, 10 hours:

```
20 × (3600 / 25) × 10 = 28,800 calls/day
```
