AstraDial is a voice and sales automation platform for call management, voice bots, and lead tracking.

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up with your company email">
    Head over to [app.astradial.com](https://app.astradial.com) and create an account using your **official company email address**.

    Verify your email by clicking the confirmation link sent to your inbox.

    <Info>
      Personal email addresses (Gmail, Yahoo, etc.) are not accepted. Use your company domain email to register.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete KYC">
    After signing in, complete the Know Your Customer (KYC) process from your dashboard. This is a one-time verification required before your account can be activated.

    You will need to provide:
    - Business registration details
    - Authorized contact information
    - Use case description
  </Step>

  <Step title="Account approval and DID number">
    Once KYC is verified, the AstraDial team will approve your account and reach out to you with:

    - A **DID number** (Direct Inward Dialing) assigned to your account
    - A **free trial** period post a demo session with our team

    <Info>
      Allow time for the team to set up the infrastructure for your account. This includes telephony routing, voice bot provisioning, and dashboard configuration.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Infrastructure setup">
    Our team configures the backend infrastructure for your account — telephony, call routing, bot setup, and integrations.

    Once everything is ready, you will receive a **confirmation message on WhatsApp** with your account details and next steps.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log in and explore">
    Log in to [app.astradial.com](https://app.astradial.com) and start using the platform.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Features

### Dashboard

Your dashboard shows:
- **Total calls** — aggregate call volume across all channels
- **Success rate** — percentage of calls that connected
- **Conversion ratio** — leads converted from calls
- **Version** — current platform version

### Call logs

Every call is logged with full details:
- Play back **call recordings** from the dashboard
- **Tag calls** with custom labels (e.g., "Interested", "Follow-up", "Not Reachable")
- Create, update, and delete tags as your workflow evolves

<Tip>
  Tags let your team quickly classify and act on calls without reading full transcriptions.
</Tip>

### Voice bot

AstraDial offers two voice bot options:
- **Astra GenAI** — conversational AI bot for natural interactions
- **Astra Lite** — lightweight bot for structured call flows
- Configure system prompts, define functions, and set up call transfer rules

### Lead management

Centralize leads from every source:
- **Facebook & Meta Ads** — auto-capture leads from ad campaigns
- **Web applications** — receive leads via webhook from your website or app
- **HMS & other platforms** — integrate with any system using the webhook API
- Track lead status, source, and history in one unified view
