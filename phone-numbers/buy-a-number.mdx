## What is a phone number?

A phone number (also called a DID) is the number that your customers dial to reach you. When someone calls this number, the call arrives at your Astradial platform and gets routed however you set it up — to a person, a team, an AI bot, or a menu.

You need at least one phone number to start receiving calls.

<Info>
  DID stands for "Direct Inward Dialing." You will see this term used in telecom, but it just means "a phone number people can call."
</Info>

## How to buy a number

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Phone Numbers page">
    In the left sidebar, click **Deploy**, then click **Phone Numbers**.

    You will land on the Phone Numbers page. At the top, you will see three stat cards:

    - **Active Numbers** — numbers you already own and are using
    - **Pending Requests** — numbers you have requested but an admin has not approved yet
    - **Available to Buy** — numbers in the marketplace ready for purchase
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go to the Buy a Number tab">
    Click the **Buy a Number** tab at the top of the page.

    You will see a list of all available phone numbers. They are sorted by price, cheapest first.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick a number">
    Each number in the list shows you four things:

    - **Number** — the actual phone number (e.g. +91 80 4567 1234)
    - **Price/mo** — what you will pay every month (e.g. ₹1,999/mo)
    - **Region** — where the number is from (e.g. Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi)
    - **Provider** — the telecom carrier (e.g. Tata)

    <Tip>
      Look for the **Best Value** badge. Numbers with this badge are the cheapest available in their region.
    </Tip>

    Browse the list and find a number that works for you.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Request the number">
    Click the **Request** button next to the number you want.

    A success message will appear:

    > "Number requested, awaiting admin approval."

    That is it — you have submitted your request.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for admin approval">
    Your requested number now moves to the **Pending Requests** section on the **My Numbers** tab.

    An admin on your organization will review your request and approve it. You do not need to do anything else.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start using your number">
    Once approved, the number appears on your **My Numbers** tab with a status of **Active**.

    You can now configure routing so calls to this number go where you want them to. See [Configure call routing](/phone-numbers/configure-routing) for next steps.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  You cannot buy a number and start receiving calls instantly. Every request goes through admin approval first. This usually takes less than a day.
</Note>

## What happens after approval?

Once your number is active, you need to set up **routing** — this tells the system what to do when someone calls the number. Without routing, calls to your number will not go anywhere.

Head to [Configure call routing](/phone-numbers/configure-routing) to set that up.
