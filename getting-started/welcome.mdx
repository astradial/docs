## What is Astradial?

Astradial is a cloud phone system. That means instead of buying expensive phone hardware and dealing with wires and cables, you get a full business phone system that runs entirely in your web browser.

With Astradial, you can:

- **Get professional phone numbers** for your business
- **Receive and make calls** from anywhere with an internet connection
- **Route calls** to the right person or team automatically
- **Track every call** with full history, recordings, and analytics
- **Manage customers** with a built-in mini CRM
- **Deploy AI bots** that can answer calls and talk to your customers
- **Create support tickets** from calls so nothing falls through the cracks
- **Automate workflows** like sending WhatsApp messages after a missed call

You manage everything from a single dashboard at [editor.astradial.com](https://editor.astradial.com).

## Key features

Here is a quick look at what you get with Astradial.

### Phone numbers

Buy local phone numbers (called DIDs) and assign them to your organisation. When someone calls that number, Astradial handles the rest -- routing the call to the right person or queue.

### Users & extensions

Add your team members as users. Each user gets their own extension number (like 1001, 1002, etc.) so they can receive calls. You control who can do what with roles and permissions.

### Call queues

A queue is a waiting line for callers. When all your agents are busy, callers wait in the queue and hear hold music until someone is free. You decide how calls get distributed -- round robin, longest idle, or random.

### Call history & recordings

Every single call is logged. You can see who called, when, how long the call lasted, and whether it was answered. If you turn on recording, you can listen back to any call.

### Mini CRM

Track your customers without needing a separate CRM tool. Add clients, manage leads, track deals through stages, and see the full history of every interaction -- all inside Astradial.

### AI bots

Deploy AI-powered voice bots that can answer calls, have conversations with callers, collect information, and route them to the right person. Set up bots with custom workflows so they say exactly what you need.

### Tickets

When a call needs follow-up, create a ticket. Assign it to a team member, set a priority, and track it until it is resolved. Tickets can also be created automatically from missed calls.

### Workflows & automation

Build automations that trigger on events. For example: when a call is missed, automatically send a WhatsApp message to the caller. Or when a new lead comes in, assign it to a sales rep.
