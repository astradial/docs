This guide walks you through every step of creating your Astradial account -- from signing up to getting approved and logging into your dashboard for the first time.

The whole process takes about 5 minutes of your time, plus a short wait for admin approval.

<Info>
  You only need to do this once. After your account is set up and approved, you just sign in with your email and password every time.
</Info>

## Create your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Astradial dashboard">
    Open your web browser (we recommend Chrome) and go to:

    ```
    https://editor.astradial.com
    ```

    You will see a sign-in page with two tabs at the top: **Sign In** and **Create Account**.
  </Step>

  <Step title='Click the "Create Account" tab'>
    At the top of the sign-in box, you will see two tabs. Click on the one that says **Create Account**.

    The form will change to show you the sign-up fields.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your email and password">
    Fill in the two fields:

    - **Email** -- Type your email address. This will be your login email, so use one you check regularly.
    - **Password** -- Choose a password. It must be at least **6 characters** long.

    <Tip>
      Use a strong password that mixes letters, numbers, and symbols. You will use this password every time you sign in.
    </Tip>

    Once both fields are filled in, click the **Create Account** button.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check your email for the verification link">
    After you click Create Account, Astradial sends a verification email to the address you entered.

    Open your email inbox and look for an email from Astradial. It will contain a verification link.

    **Click the verification link in the email.**

    This tells Astradial that you own this email address and it is real.

    <Warning>
      If you do not see the email, check your **Spam** or **Junk** folder. Sometimes email providers accidentally put verification emails there.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go back to the sign-in page">
    After clicking the verification link, go back to:

    ```
    https://editor.astradial.com
    ```

    You should now be on the **Sign In** tab (not Create Account).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign in with your email and password">
    Enter the same email and password you used when creating your account, then click **Sign In**.

    Since this is your very first time logging in, Astradial knows you do not have an organisation yet. Instead of taking you to the dashboard, it will show you a setup form.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Set up your organisation

When you sign in for the first time, you will see a page titled **Set Up Your Organisation**. This is where you tell us about your business so we can get you set up properly.

<Note>
  An "organisation" in Astradial is your company or team. All your users, phone numbers, calls, and settings live under your organisation.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Fill in your organisation details">
    You will see a form with several fields. Fill in each one:

    | Field | What to enter |
    | --- | --- |
    | **Organisation Name** | The name of your company or team. For example: "Sunrise Hotels" or "Acme Support". |
    | **Phone** | A contact phone number for your organisation. This is just for our records, not your Astradial phone number. |
    | **Address** | Your business address. |
    | **Industry** | Click the dropdown and pick the industry that best matches your business (e.g. Hospitality, Healthcare, Retail, etc.). |
    | **Company Size** | Select how many people are in your company. |
    | **Expected Users** | How many people on your team will use Astradial? This helps us set things up for you. |
    | **Description** | A brief sentence or two about what your business does. For example: "We are a boutique hotel chain with 3 locations in Mumbai." |

    <Tip>
      Do not worry about getting everything perfect. You can update your organisation details later from the dashboard settings.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title='Click "Submit Application"'>
    Once all the fields are filled in, click the **Submit Application** button at the bottom of the form.

    Your application is now sent to the Astradial team for review.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Wait for approval

After you submit your application, you will see a message on screen that says your organisation is **awaiting approval**.

<Info>
  This approval step lets us make sure every organisation on Astradial is a real business. We review applications quickly -- usually within a few hours during business hours.
</Info>

While you are waiting:

- You **do not** need to keep the browser open. You can close it and come back later.
- You **cannot** access the dashboard yet. If you try to sign in, you will see the "awaiting approval" message again.
- You will receive an email once your organisation is approved.

## Sign in after approval

Once your organisation is approved, here is how you get into your dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to editor.astradial.com">
    Open your browser and go to:

    ```
    https://editor.astradial.com
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign in with your email and password">
    Enter the same email and password you created earlier, then click **Sign In**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="You are in!">
    This time, instead of seeing the setup form or the waiting message, you will land on your **dashboard**. This is your home base in Astradial.

    You are now ready to start setting up your phone system.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I did not receive the verification email">
    1. Check your **Spam** or **Junk** folder.
    2. Make sure you typed your email address correctly when signing up.
    3. Wait a few minutes -- sometimes emails take a moment to arrive.
    4. If it still has not arrived after 10 minutes, try creating your account again with the same email.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I forgot my password">
    On the sign-in page, look for a **Forgot Password** link. Click it, enter your email, and you will receive a password reset link in your inbox.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My application has been pending for a long time">
    Approvals usually happen within a few hours during business hours. If it has been more than 24 hours, send us an email at **support@astradial.com** with your organisation name and we will look into it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I see an error when trying to sign in">
    Make sure you are using the correct email and password. If you recently changed your password, use the new one. If the problem continues, email **support@astradial.com** with a screenshot of the error.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
