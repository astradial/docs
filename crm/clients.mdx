## What are clients?

In Astradial, a "client" is a **company** — a business that you work with. Each company can have multiple **contacts** (the individual people at that company), multiple **deals**, and a full history of calls and activity.

Think of it this way:

- **Company** = the business itself (e.g. "Grand Estancia Hotel")
- **Contacts** = the people at that company (e.g. "Ravi Kumar, Front Desk Manager")

## Opening the Clients page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Mini CRM in the sidebar">
    In the left sidebar, find the **Mini CRM** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Clients">
    Click **Clients**. You will land on the Clients page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The stats bar

At the top of the Clients page, you will see four stat cards that give you a quick summary:

| Card | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Companies** | The total number of companies in your CRM. |
| **Contacts** | The total number of individual contacts across all companies. |
| **Open Deals** | How many active deals are linked to your companies. |
| **Pipeline Value** | The total money value of all your open deals added together. |

<Tip>
  Glance at these cards every morning to get a quick snapshot of your CRM health. If the Pipeline Value is growing, your sales team is doing well.
</Tip>

## Adding a company

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Add Company">
    On the Clients page, click the **Add Company** button. A form appears.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in the company details">
    The form has the following fields:

    | Field | What to enter | Required? |
    |---|---|---|
    | **Company Name** | The name of the business (e.g. "Grand Estancia Hotel") | Yes |
    | **Industry** | What kind of business it is (e.g. "Hospitality", "Technology") | No |
    | **Company Size** | How many employees (e.g. "50-100") | No |
    | **Phone** | The company's main phone number | No |
    | **Email** | The company's main email address | No |
    | **Website** | The company's website URL | No |
    | **Address** | The physical address of the business | No |
    | **Notes** | Anything else you want to remember about this company | No |

    Only the company name is required. Fill in as much or as little as you like — you can always come back and add more later.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Save">
    Click the **Save** button at the bottom of the form. The company is now added to your CRM and appears in the table.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If you add the company's phone number, all calls from that number will automatically link to this company in your call history. This is one of the biggest time-savers in the CRM.
</Note>

## Viewing company details

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click a company row">
    On the Clients page, click any company row in the table. A **detail panel** slides open on the right side of the screen.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Browse the detail panel">
    The panel shows you everything about this company:

    - **Company information** — name, industry, size, phone, email, website, address, notes
    - **Contacts** — all the individual people at this company
    - **Deals** — all deals linked to this company, with their current stage and value
    - **Activity** — a timeline of recent interactions, including calls, status changes, and notes
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  The activity timeline updates automatically. When someone on your team calls a number linked to this company, that call appears in the activity section without anyone needing to log it manually.
</Info>

## Assigning a company to a team member

If you want a specific person on your team to be responsible for a company, you can assign it to them.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the company in the table">
    Locate the company row on the Clients page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the actions menu">
    On the right side of the company row, click the **three-dot menu** (the **...** icon). A dropdown menu appears.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Assign">
    Select **Assign** from the dropdown. A list of your team members appears.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick a team member">
    Click the name of the person you want to assign this company to. They are now the owner of this account and will see it highlighted in their view.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Assigning companies prevents confusion. When everyone knows who is responsible for which client, nothing gets duplicated and no customer gets ignored.
</Tip>

## Editing a company

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the actions menu">
    On the company row, click the **three-dot menu** (**...**).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Edit">
    Select **Edit** from the dropdown. The company form opens with all the current details filled in.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Make your changes and save">
    Update any fields you need to change, then click **Save**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Deleting a company

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the actions menu">
    On the company row, click the **three-dot menu** (**...**).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Delete">
    Select **Delete** from the dropdown.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm the deletion">
    A confirmation dialog appears asking if you are sure. Click **Delete** to confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Deleting a company removes it and all its linked contacts from the CRM. Deals linked to the company will be unlinked. This action cannot be undone. Make sure you really want to delete before confirming.
</Warning>
