## What is a CRM?

CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management. In plain terms, it is a place to keep track of all your customers, the people who might become customers, and the business deals you are working on.

Think of it like a digital address book, but much smarter. Instead of just storing names and phone numbers, a CRM stores the full history of every interaction — who called, what was discussed, what deals are in progress, and what needs follow-up.

## Why is the CRM built into Astradial?

Most CRM tools are separate products. You log calls in one place, track customers in another, and waste time switching between screens.

With Astradial, your phone system and CRM live together in the same dashboard. That means:

- When a customer calls, their CRM record pops up automatically
- Call history is linked to contacts — you see every call a customer has ever made
- Your team can track leads and deals without leaving the platform
- No need to buy, set up, or learn a separate CRM tool

<Info>
  We call it "Mini CRM" because it covers the essentials — clients, leads, and deals. It is not trying to be a massive enterprise CRM with hundreds of features. It gives you what you actually need for day-to-day customer management.
</Info>

## How to get to the Mini CRM

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the sidebar">
    Look at the left side of your screen. The sidebar shows all the sections of the platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the Mini CRM section">
    Scroll down in the sidebar until you see the **Mini CRM** section. Under it, you will find links to **Clients**, **Leads**, **Deals**, and **Customize**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click any page to get started">
    Click **Clients** to manage your companies and contacts, **Leads** to work your lead pipeline, **Deals** to track business opportunities, or **Customize** to set up custom fields and pipeline stages.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How the CRM connects to your phone system

This is the biggest advantage of having your CRM inside your phone platform. Here is what happens automatically:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Calls link to contacts" icon="link">
    When you add a customer's phone number to their CRM record, every call from that number is automatically linked to their profile. Open a contact and see their full call history.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Click-to-call from CRM" icon="phone-arrow-up-right">
    See a contact's phone number in the CRM? Click it to call them directly. No dialling, no copy-pasting numbers.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  Start by adding your most important customers to the Clients section. Once their phone numbers are in the system, you will immediately see the connection between your CRM records and your call logs.
</Tip>
