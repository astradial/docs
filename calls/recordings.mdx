---
title: "Recordings"
description: "Listen to and download call recordings from the call history."
---

Astradial can record phone calls automatically. Recordings are stored on your server and accessible from the call history.

## Listen to a recording

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Calls">
    Click **Calls** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the call">
    Look for calls that have a **Play** button in the Recording column. Only calls with actual conversation time have recordings — missed calls or unanswered calls do not.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Play">
    Click the play button. An audio player appears at the bottom of the screen showing:
    - The caller and receiver numbers
    - Call time and duration
    - Play/pause controls
    - A progress bar you can drag to seek
    - A download button
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Download a recording

Click the **Download** button in the recording column or in the audio player. The recording file will download to your computer.

<Note>
  Download is only available to **Owner** and **Admin** roles. Managers can listen but cannot download.
</Note>

## Who can access recordings

| Action | Owner | Admin | Manager | Agent |
|--------|-------|-------|---------|-------|
| Listen (play) | Yes | Yes | Yes | No |
| Download | Yes | Yes | No | No |
| Delete | Yes | Yes | No | No |

If you see a disabled play button with a tooltip saying "You don't have permission to access recordings", your role does not have recording access.

## When are calls recorded?

Calls are recorded when:
- Recording is enabled for the user's extension (set in user settings)
- The call is answered and has conversation time
- The recording system is working and has disk space

Calls are **not** recorded when:
- The call was not answered (NO ANSWER status)
- Recording is disabled for that extension
- The call had zero talk time
