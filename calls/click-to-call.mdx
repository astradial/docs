## What is click-to-call?

Click-to-call lets you start a phone call directly from the Astradial web interface. Instead of opening a softphone app, looking up a number, and dialing it manually, you just click a button and Astradial connects the call for you.

Think of it like a magic button. You click it, your phone rings, you pick up, and then the other person's phone rings. Once they pick up, you are in a conversation. That is it.

## How it works

Here is what happens behind the scenes when you use click-to-call:

1. You click the call button and choose who to call
2. Astradial calls **your** extension first (your softphone rings)
3. You pick up your softphone
4. Astradial then calls the **other person**
5. When they pick up, you are both connected

<Info>
  Your softphone needs to be registered and working for click-to-call to work. Astradial calls your extension first, so if your softphone is not connected, the call will not go through.
</Info>

## Making a call

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Calls or Tickets page">
    You can start a click-to-call from the **Calls** page or the **Tickets** page. Look for the **phone icon** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the phone icon">
    Click the phone icon to open the call dialog.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your extension">
    In the **From** field, select your extension. This is the phone that will ring first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the destination">
    In the **To** field, enter the number you want to call. This can be:
    - A full phone number (e.g. +91 98765 43210)
    - Another extension in your organisation (e.g. 1002)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Call">
    Hit the **Call** button. Your softphone will ring. Pick it up, and Astradial will connect you to the other party.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If you are on the Tickets page and a ticket has a phone number attached, you can click-to-call that number directly without typing it in. Astradial fills in the number for you.
</Tip>

## Things to know

<Note>
  Click-to-call uses your extension and softphone. Make sure your softphone app (Zoiper, Opal, etc.) is open and registered before you try to make a call. If it is not connected, Astradial cannot ring your phone.
</Note>

<Warning>
  If you click **Call** and nothing happens, check the following:
  - Is your softphone app open and registered?
  - Is the "To" number correct and complete?
  - Does your role allow making outbound calls?
</Warning>

## Who can use click-to-call?

All roles can use click-to-call -- Owners, Admins, Managers, and Agents. If you have a registered softphone and a valid extension, you can make calls from the dashboard.
